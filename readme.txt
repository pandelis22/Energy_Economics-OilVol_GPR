Follow the steps below:

1) open the "main" matlab script
2) adjust the inputs if needed (e.g., the h - forecasting horizon)
3) run the script and the forecasts, errors and the MSPE of the most important models of the study will be computed.

paper is cited as:

Delis, P., Degiannakis, S., Filis, G. (2025). Navigating crude oil volatility forecasts: Assessing the contribution of geopolitical risk. Energy Economics, 2025, 148, 108594. (https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0140988325004189)
